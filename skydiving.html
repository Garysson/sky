Tandem Skydiving

What is a tandem skydive?

A tandem skydive is the quickest and easiest way to experience the thrill of freefall at over 120 miles per hour, while securely harnessed to one of our experienced and accomplished instructors. Adrenaline enthusiasts can live this adventure at our two world-class locations, the Palm Dropzone and the Desert Dropzone. 

Every customer who purchases a tandem package at either of our locations will receive photographs and our standard edited video of their experience, via email, within 48 hours of completing their skydive. The tandem package also includes medical insurance. 

Located within the city of Dubai, the highlight of a tandem experience at our Palm Dropzone is the stunning view of the iconic Palm Jumeirah island. Not to mention other landmarks such as the World Islands, Ain Dubai and the Burj al Arab. 

Our Desert Dropzone, located about 35 km outside Dubai, offers customers a skydive over Dubai’s sandy dunes. A desert safari, if you will, like no other. 

You can also pay to receive an Instagram-friendly video of your skydive. This 1-minute edited video features all the highlights of your tandem skydive - perfect for sharing (and bragging about) with friends and family as soon as you receive it. 

Those interested in a tandem experience at either location must meet all of our Requirements (listed below). If you do not meet all our requirements you will not be allowed to skydive and your payment will not be refunded. We also suggest you read through the Important section (listed below) for information on how to prepare for your skydive and for what to expect on the day. For any other queries please contact our tandem reservation team directly.

We look forward to skydiving with you!

Requirements

A minimum degree of fitness is required to complete a tandem skydive and to ensure a safe landing for you and your instructor. Your Body Mass Index (BMI) is an indicator of this.

Skydive Dubai requires each tandem customer to meet both, the weight and BMI requirements listed below. These are the equipment’s manufacturer’s requirements and we cannot make exceptions for safety reasons.

Women

Weight: 90.0 kg/ 198.0 lbs or less.
BMI: 27.5 or less.

Men

Weight: 100.0 kg/220 lbs or less.
BMI: 30.0 or less.

There is no minimum weight requirement in order to be eligible to skydive. Please note, our staff will weigh you (with your shoes and clothes on) when you check-in on the day of your skydive. You are welcome to visit our locations before you book your skydive to weigh yourself on our scales.

You must be between 18 to 75 years of age (according to the Gregorian calendar) on the day of your tandem skydive. Please bring a valid and official government photo ID (Emirates ID, driver’s license, passport) on the day of your skydive.

If you are 70 years old or older on the day you skydive, kindly download and fill out the Declaration of Fitness form (point 6) prior to your arrival at our location. The form must be completed and must be signed and stamped by your doctor.

Skydive Dubai has a zero-tolerance alcohol and/or drugs policy. There must be no alcohol or drugs in your system 24 hours before your skydive.

If you suffer from or have previously suffered from any of the conditions listed below, kindly download and fill out the Declaration of Fitness form (point 6) prior to your arrival at our location. The form must be completed and must be signed and stamped by your doctor. This is essential for both your safety and the safety of your instructor. If you do not follow these instructions, there is a chance your skydive could be canceled. Please contact your doctor in case of any queries.

  • Epilepsy
  • Fits
  • Severe head injury
  • Recurrent blackouts or giddiness
  • Disease of the brain or nervous system
  • High blood pressure
  • Heart or lung disease
  • Dislocated shoulder or other limb(s)
  • Recurrent weakness
  • Diabetes
  • Mental illness
  • Drug or alcohol addiction
  • Paralysis 
  • Any past or present medical conditions


If you have a prosthetic limb, you are requested to contact our reservations team before you book your tandem experience. Please email them at info@skydivedubai.ae. 

If you are pregnant you should not skydive.

Please avoid scuba diving at least 24 hours prior to you tandem skydive.

The time mentioned in your booking confirmation email is your arrival time, it is not your skydive time. If you selected a slot of 10:00 am, please arrive at 10:00 am.

Important: Our team does try to get customers ready for their skydive as soon as possible. However, sometimes, circumstances beyond our control (eg: air traffic control, unfavorable weather conditions, etc) may cause a delay in the day's schedule thereby increasing the customer's wait time. This is why we suggest that you plan to spend between three to four hours (i.e. from the time you check-in till the time you complete your skydive) at our locations on the day of your skydive.

  • Your gift certificate, if using it.
  • A completed and stamped Declaration of Fitness form if you are 70 years or older, or if you have or have had any medical conditions mentioned in the list above.

Please dress suitably and comfortably for your skydive and be respectful of the local culture and sentiments.

  • Athletic, comfortable clothes are advised. T-shirts/tops with sleeves and shorts that cover the thigh area are recommended so that the harness fits comfortably.
  • Collared shirts should be avoided. (Flapping collars during freefall can be unpleasant.)
  • Avoid wearing white or light-colored shorts or pants as they will get dirty. 
  • Laced-up sneakers or sports shoes are ideal for your skydive. Please make sure your shoes don't have any buckles or studs on them.
  • Not allowed: Swimwear.
  • Not allowed: Sandals, boots, open-toe, or heeled/platform shoes of any type.
  • Customers are advised to remove any precious jewelry, watches, and/or other accessories before their skydive. These can be placed in the free personal lockers at our locations.
  • Every customer will be given protective eye goggles to wear during their skydive. So, if you need to wear prescription glasses or lenses during your skydive, please do so. You don't want to miss that view!

Skydiving is a weather-dependent sport. High-speed winds, poor visibility, rain (yes, it is known to happen in Dubai) and low clouds, are just some of the reasons that can delay, or even cancel our skydiving operations.

In case of bad weather, our team will announce a “standby”, which means we have to put our operations on hold. Regular announcements will be made updating you on the conditions while you wait at our location. 

If the weather shows no signs of improving and we have to cancel operations and you cannot reschedule for the next available date, you will receive a refund.

While we always do our best to communicate operational changes, Skydive Dubai reserves the right to close our dropzones without any prior warning. In this case, our team will contact you by phone or email to cancel or reschedule your skydive.

Skydive Dubai is not liable for any costs (travel, stay, etc.) incurred by the individual in the instances mentioned above. Please visit our Sales & Refund Policy for information.

Reservations must be made through the live booking calendar on our official website.

Desert Dropzone: If you haven’t booked in advance and would like to skydive, you can visit our locations and put your name on the daily waiting list. Once you add your name to the list our staff will give you a time at which to return. Please note, this list is on a first-come-first-served basis and there are no guarantees you will get a turn. It depends on the day’s bookings and scheduling.

Each person who would like to skydive must personally add their name to the list. Skydive Dubai staff or the person's friends/family are not allowed to add names on their behalf. 

Important: Customers whose bookings may have been affected/canceled by Skydive Dubai due to weather conditions and/or other unforeseen circumstances, will have priority on the waitlist, if applicable.

Each slot can be purchased for a last-minute priority fee of AED 700. The customer will have to pay this fee in addition to the price of a regular tandem.

Once purchased, you will be guaranteed the next available tandem slot on the day. You will also receive our standard edited video, photos and insurance as part of the regular tandem package.

Important: The customer must meet all our tandem requirements to be eligible for this option.

Every tandem customer will be accompanied by a camera flyer capturing every moment of the skydive. A tandem package at both our locations includes photographs and a standard edited video (about 3 minutes long) of your tandem experience. Customers can purchase a 1-minute Instagram video edit for an additional fee. This video edit will include the highlights of your skydive.

Customers can also purchase the add-on ‘selfie-view’ footage which will be captured by your tandem instructor using a wrist-mounted camera during your skydive. This extra footage is available for an extra fee and will be incorporated into the standard edited video, and the 1-minute Instagram video edit, if purchased. 

While our camera flyers are highly trained and use the latest equipment, given the nature of the activity, on the rare occasion that the camera equipment used during a tandem skydive malfunctions or there is a technical glitch that is beyond our control, or the weather affects the overall outcome of the tandem media, the incident will be escalated to Skydive Dubai’s management for reviewal and the customer may be eligible for a refund of the money paid for the camera package in question. i.e AED 300 only for the standard photos and video, or AED 250 only for the selfie-view add-on. Please note, the decision of the management team will be final.
